Termux Setup Wizard

Click each button in order. The UI does everything via ADB automatically — no typing on the car screen needed.

1
Install Termux via ADB
Automated ▶

Downloads Termux from F-Droid and installs it via ADB. Make sure ADB is connected (green dot).

💡 Uses the Termux APK from the App Store. Or drag your own APK into the custom install zone in the 🏪 App Store tab.
2
Grant Termux permissions
Automated ▶

Grants Termux the RUN_COMMAND permission so the UI can execute commands inside it via ADB.

3
Run start.sh in Termux
Automated ▶

Sends start.sh to Termux via RUN_COMMAND intent — installs Node.js, git, ADB, clones the repo and starts the server.

💡 From now on start.sh auto-runs every time Termux opens. No USB or SSH needed.
4
Auto-enable ADB on every boot
Automated ▶

Installs termux-boot and writes a boot script that automatically re-enables ADB and starts the server every time the car restarts — no manual steps ever again.

How it works: Android boots → Termux:Boot runs ~/.termux/boot/start.sh → ADB re-enabled on port 5555 → Node server starts → UI connects automatically.
⚠ Requires: Grant Termux:Boot the Run at startup permission from Android Settings → Apps → Termux:Boot → Permissions after installation.

Enable ADB on BYD Sealion 7

The Sealion 7 runs a locked-down DiLink OS. Follow these steps to unlock ADB and connect your device. Do this while parked.

1
Enable ADB on the BYD Sealion 7
On the car screen ▶

Follow these steps exactly on the car's touchscreen:

1
Open Settings
Tap the gear icon on the home screen or swipe down from the top bar.
2
Go to System
Scroll down and tap System in the settings list.
3
Tap Version
Inside System, tap Version to open the version info screen.
4
Press "Restore Factory" 10 times
Find the Restore Factory button and tap it 10 times rapidly. A hidden developer menu will appear. Do NOT confirm any factory reset prompt.
5
Rotate the screen
The hidden menu may require rotating the display orientation to reveal all options. Tap the rotate button if present.
6
Tap "Connect USB"
In the hidden developer menu, tap Connect USB. ADB is now enabled. Plug a USB cable from the car's data port to your laptop.
⚠ Do not tap "Restore Factory" and confirm — just tap it 10 times fast to trigger the hidden menu. Do not confirm any reset dialog.
💡 Tip: Use the car's USB-A data port (usually in the center console), not the USB-C charging port. If the PC doesn't detect the device, try a different USB cable or laptop port.

Then verify the connection from your PC:

adb devices

You should see the Sealion 7 listed as an ADB device. No RSA prompt will appear on the car screen — it connects silently.
