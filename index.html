Termux Setup

Download Termux from F-Droid (not Google Play), then run one command to start the server.

1
Download Termux from F-Droid
On device ▶
⚠ Do not use Google Play — the Termux version there is outdated and broken. Use F-Droid instead.

Open the car browser and go to:

https://f-droid.org/en/packages/com.termux/

Download and install the APK directly on the device.

2
Connect ADB from your PC
On PC ▶

Plug a USB cable from the car's data port to your PC. Then run:

adb devices

Or connect wirelessly over TCP:

adb connect <car-ip>:5555
💡 Find the car's IP at Settings → WiFi → tap the connected network. Once connected the status indicators in the header will turn green.
3
Or install via ADB from your PC
On PC ▶

Download the Termux APK on your PC from F-Droid, then sideload it via ADB:

# Download from F-Droid (or use the App Store tab above) adb install -r com.termux_118.apk

Or use the 🏪 App Store tab — drop the Termux APK in the custom install zone to sideload it wirelessly.

💡 Once installed, launch Termux from the car screen or via ADB:

adb shell am start -n com.termux/.HomeActivity
3
Run this one command in Termux
In Termux ▶

Open Termux and paste this single command — it installs everything and starts the server:

curl -s https://raw.githubusercontent.com/aliboumrah/Sealion7Store/main/start.sh -o ~/start.sh && bash ~/start.sh
💡 Next time just run: bash ~/start.sh

Enable ADB on BYD Sealion 7

The Sealion 7 runs a locked-down DiLink OS. Follow these steps to unlock ADB and connect your device. Do this while parked.

1
Enable ADB on the BYD Sealion 7
On the car screen ▶

Follow these steps exactly on the car's touchscreen:

1
Open Settings
Tap the gear icon on the home screen or swipe down from the top bar.
2
Go to System
Scroll down and tap System in the settings list.
3
Tap Version
Inside System, tap Version to open the version info screen.
4
Press "Restore Factory" 10 times
Find the Restore Factory button and tap it 10 times rapidly. A hidden developer menu will appear. Do NOT confirm any factory reset prompt.
5
Rotate the screen
The hidden menu may require rotating the display orientation to reveal all options. Tap the rotate button if present.
6
Tap "Connect USB"
In the hidden developer menu, tap Connect USB. ADB is now enabled. Plug a USB cable from the car's data port to your laptop.
⚠ Do not tap "Restore Factory" and confirm — just tap it 10 times fast to trigger the hidden menu. Do not confirm any reset dialog.
💡 Tip: Use the car's USB-A data port (usually in the center console), not the USB-C charging port. If the PC doesn't detect the device, try a different USB cable or laptop port.

Then verify the connection from your PC:

adb devices

You should see the Sealion 7 listed as an ADB device. No RSA prompt will appear on the car screen — it connects silently.
